What We Do

At Samsung, we take pride in our diverse portfolio of approximately 80 companies, spanning across various sectors including, but not limited to, finance, medical services, shipbuilding, construction, and, most notably, electronics.

Our commitment extends beyond delivering high-quality premium devices, as we also offer a wide range of software-based services. These services encompass device configuration, maintenance, advanced security features, and privacy management, all integrated within our devices.
However, our ultimate goal goes beyond providing products and services. We aspire to empower our consumers to transcend boundaries and accomplish what was once considered unimaginable. Through our innovative devices and the seamless interaction between our smart home appliances, we aim to enable users to unlock their true potential.

Rest assured, Samsung is your comprehensive solution, catering to all your needs and desires.
